Our Services

Making shipping easier, faster, and perfectly protected.

Conestoga
Flatbed Shipping

Our Conestoga trailers are designed to make shipping easier. You get the flexibility of a flatbed, but with a sliding tarp system that protects your freight without slowing things down. That means quicker loads, fewer delays, and less hassle for you.

Standard Flatbed

  • Requires tarping
  • Takes more time to load
  • Limited weather protection

Conestoga Trailer

  • No tarping needed
  • Faster loading
  • Full protection from weather

Why It Matters

Choosing the right trailer can save you time and money. With Conestoga shipping, you get:

✔️ Faster deliveries
✔️ Lower labor costs
