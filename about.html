Our Story

Moving freight the right way since 2005.

At Koval Trucking, we’re a family-run company. We believe in doing things the right way — showing up on time, communicating clearly, and delivering your load safely.

We specialize in Conestoga flatbed shipping, which gives you the flexibility of a flatbed without the hassle of tarping or worrying about weather damage. We know that your time is valuable, and delays cost money. That's why we invested in the best equipment to ensure every run goes smoothly.

When you work with us, you’re not dealing with a middleman or a broker — you’re working directly with the carrier. That means honest communication, direct answers, and reliable service every single time.
