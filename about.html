Our Story

We are Koval Trucking & HD Fleet Service.

A family-owned transportation company built on dependable freight solutions, specialized West Coast lanes, and equipment maintained to a high standard.

A regional specialist with a long-term view.

For over 21 years, Koval Trucking has built its reputation around straightforward communication, consistent service, and freight handled with care. We focus on flatbed and Conestoga freight throughout the West Coast so customers get route experience, specialized handling, and efficient delivery planning.

Our logistics model combines regional expertise with real-time decision making. When weather, traffic, or timing changes affect a load, we respond quickly to keep freight on the best possible route.

What makes us different is the support behind the trucks. Our full-service HD Fleet shop helps maintain safer, road-ready equipment and reduces mechanical downtime before it becomes a customer problem.

West Coast Flatbed & Conestoga

We focus on the equipment types and lanes we know best, helping customers move specialized freight with more precision and fewer surprises.

The HD Fleet Service Advantage

  • Expert maintenance for heavy-duty equipment.
  • Minimized downtime through in-house repair control.

Direct Carrier Accountability
