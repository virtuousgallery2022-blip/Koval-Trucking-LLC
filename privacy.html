Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

At Koval Trucking, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy. This policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you visit the website, and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us through the contact forms on our website, such as your name, company name, email address, phone number, and freight details. This allows us to provide you with shipping quotes and customer service.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website.
  • Communicate with you regarding quotes, support, and services.
  • Send emails or respond to your directly initiated contact.

4. Data Security

We have implemented measures designed to secure your personal information from accidental loss and from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure. However, no data transmission over the Internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
